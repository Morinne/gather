Welcome to Gather

A beer garden for ALL

Gather is a place for all ages and all types of people to gather, get to know each other, and have fun.

We have a large, centralized pavilion for mixing and mingling, as well as a variety of gazebos for groups of any size.

If you're looking for new friends, join one of our many group meetups, like the weekly board game group or musician jam. Or if you already have a group, reserve one of our gazebos for your next meetup.

We have a variety of craft beers, ciders, and mead, along with a tasty selection finger-foods to satisfy the munchies.
